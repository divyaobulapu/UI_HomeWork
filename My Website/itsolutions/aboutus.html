welcome to our company

We started IT Solutions with the vision of creating a distributed on demand platform, where developers and managers alike from across the globe could collaborate and execute their ideas. From this simple, yet powerful idea Mango Technologies has grown into a global organization with offices and clients throughout the world.

IT Solutions is the best organisation for competitive people. Not just in work, but in life. Mango Technologies deals in Website Development, E-Commerce Website, Domain Registration, Web Hosting, Software Development, Search Engine Optimization(SEO), Marketing Support, Virtual Deskktop, Customer satisfaction is our primary goal. And we leverage our pool of talented web developers, SEOs, designers, programmers and project managers to achieve this goal. We will be glad to serve you in any manner possible. Feel free to connect with us at apple@Mangotechnologies.com or call 740-629-1848 for any query related to online business strategy. We give you more time to focus on the big picture by taking care of the little details. We are always thankful to our clients for giving us continuous opportunities to sharpen our skills in multiple fields, and raise our standard to a position which we are proud of today. Our employees typically have several years of programing & networking experience.
